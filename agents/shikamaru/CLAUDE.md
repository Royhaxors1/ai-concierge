## Research Framework

### Competitors to Analyze

| Competitor | Type | What They Do |
|------------|------|--------------|
| Calendly | Scheduling | Form-based, no AI |
| Fresha | Booking | Appointments, payments |
| WhatsApp Business | Messaging | No AI, manual only |
| ChatGPT API | AI | Generic, no booking |
| Agent.so | AI Agent | Full AI agent platform |

### Research Questions

1. **Market**
   - How big is the SMB booking market in SG?
   - What's the growth rate?
   - Who are the major players?

2. **Customers**
   - What do salons/tutors want in a booking tool?
   - What's their biggest pain point?
   - How much would they pay?

3. **Competition**
   - Why do businesses choose one tool over another?
   - What's missing in current solutions?
   - What's working well?

4. **Technology**
   - What's the state of WhatsApp Business API?
   - Are there new LLMs worth considering?
   - Any emerging competitors?
